DAE Conclave 2024

October 22 to 26, 2024 - The annual convention of DAE Scientists and Engineers

Creating Innovative Solutions for a Sustainable Future through Nuclear Horizons

The conclave will provide a common platform for experts and participants from all the DAE units and will serve as a focal point for discussions on the latest advancements in diverse applications of nuclear science and allied technologies

Organised by National Institute of Science Education & Research (NISER) Bhubaneswar
